A Little About Us

Our polite, legally trained advisors are based in our contact centre in Pencoed, South Wales. These advisors are supported by our highly experienced management staff.

We have a network of specialist personal injury solicitors across the UK. This helps us make sure we can find the right solicitor to handle your claim. Our solicitors work on a no win no fee basis. This means that if your claim is unsuccessful, you’ll have absolutely nothing to pay.
