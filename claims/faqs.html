Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions regarding our claims process

  • The client(s)s will be contacted by a member of the claims team.once the handler has confirmed all the information and completed the quality control checklist with the client(s), they will be transferred over to a solicitor firm on our panel.

    The solicitors will send of a Conditional Fee Agreement (CFA) to the client, as well as an ID request. Once these are returned to the solicitors, the solicitors will confirm with ourselves if they will be taking on the claim.

  • The claims process can be anything between one day and 2 weeks Depending on the simplicity of the case and the cooperation of the client CFA and ID requests are usually sent out the same day as the clients information is provided to the solicitor firm.

  • CFA’s are sent out either via echo-sign or via post Echo-sign (Esign) is a digital contract, which will be sent to the clients email address. The client can sign the CFA by simply clicking on the signature space on the document and typing their name. Alternatively, CFA’s can be sent out via post if necessary.

  • We at Green Deal Finance do weekly payments on Fridays.
