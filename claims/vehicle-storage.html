Vehicle Storage

Green Deal Finance's network of secure indoor vehicle storage facilities are CCTV monitored 24 hours a day to give our clients peace of mind that their cars are in safe hands with us

Our insulated and dry stores are monitored 24 hours a day by sophisticated, insurance company approved alarm systems.All cars are insured for accidental damage, fire and theft whilst in our care.

Whatever your circumstances, you can relax in the knowledge that your vehicle is in safe hands. All cars and motorbikes are monitored with tyre checks and cleaning as standard.

However, we also recommend exercising vehicles regularly to keep engines in optimal condition

Before arranging storage, you should prepare the following details:
  • Nature of your enquiry - corporate or private.
  • Quantity of vehicles that need accommodating.
  • Type of vehicles.
  • Date on which they will arrive and for what duration.

Vehicles are fully insured against any damage whilst in the care of Green Deal Finance. Tyre checks and basic cleaning are provided as standard with valeting as an optional extra.

Vehicle exercising, MOT tests and general servicing are also available upon request.
