Roadside Recovery

Green Deal Finance has a comprehensive roadside recovery programme in place to assist you at the time of your accident. With our own fleet of recovery vehicles and our partnership with one of the largest UK vehicle recovery companies, we will never be more than 40 minutes away.

For non-fault accident victims, our recovery charges will be reclaimed from the "at fault" drivers insurance company.As a Green Deal Finance client you won’t have to pay a penny to us!

Whether your accident occurs on a country lane or in a city center location we can recover regardless of your location, our recovery drivers operate around the clock.

The early morning 3am recovery business is as normal for Green Deal Finance's as daylight hours. Our recovery vehicles are fully licensed and insured so you won't have any headaches with us.

Roadside recovery service we offer:
  • Any time of the day
  • Any location
  • Any Vehicles
What to do if your vehicle breaks down?
  • Pull on to the hard shoulder and stop as far to the left as possible with the wheels turned to the left
  • Leave your sidelights on and turn on the hazard warning lights
  • Get out of the vehicle by the left-hand door and make sure that all your passengers do the same
  • Leave animals in the vehicle or in an emergency, keep them under proper control on the verge
  • If you have reflective jackets in the vehicle wear them – do not use a warning triangle on the hard shoulder
  • Make sure that passengers keep away from the carriageway and hard shoulder and children are kept under control.
  • Don't attempt even simple repairs
